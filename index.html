Company

Integrix Design Services provides drafting support for product development, fabrication, and documentation. We help individuals and organizations convert concepts into manufacturable designs through clear models, drawings, and structured technical deliverables.

Typical deliverables include 3D models, General Arrangement (GA) drawing, and fabrication drawing.

Why Us

Designed for repeatability

We build standardized and automated design frameworks around each client’s product line, enabling faster updates, revisions, and future variants without restarting from scratch.

Lower long-term project cost

Automation and standardization are developed internally and absorbed by us—clients benefit from reduced engineering hours on future work without additional setup fees.

Lean, efficient operations

With distributed designers and minimal overhead, we deliver competitive pricing while maintaining disciplined workflows and documentation standards.

Flexible, open technical stack

Our workflows use Python-based, open-source CAD tools that we customize in-house, allowing rapid iteration and avoiding vendor lock-in.
